About Diego

Dedicated humanitarian leader, policy advocate, and nonprofit strategist.

Diego Javier Piña López, MSW, MPA

Humanitarian Leader | Policy Advocate | Nonprofit Strategist

Diego Javier Piña López is a seasoned leader with over a decade of experience in humanitarian aid, migration services, and nonprofit management. Passionate about social justice, he has dedicated his career to advocating for displaced communities, managing large-scale relief efforts, and developing innovative programs to support asylum seekers.

Leadership & Impact

Diego has held leadership roles in multiple organizations, including:

  • Researcher Content Expert, Cherokee Federal – Provides policy analysis, assesses refugee medical services, and optimizes resettlement strategies.
  • Executive Director, Migrant Services at Catholic Community Services – Led a $12M budget, 62 staff, and 600+ volunteers, securing $13M in funding and serving 1,800+ asylum seekers per night.
  • Consultant, Catholic Charities USA – Designed data collection systems and referral mechanisms to enhance migration services.
  • Senior Border Specialist, Save the Children – Allocated $7M in humanitarian aid for border response programs.

Education & Skills

  • MSW (Policy, Administration, and Community Practice) – Arizona State University
  • MPA (Nonprofit Administration) – Arizona State University
  • BA in Theater Education & BA in Religious Studies – The University of Arizona

Key skills: Strategic Planning, Fundraising, Budget Management, Data Analysis, Crisis Response, Public Speaking, Grant Writing.

Recognitions & Media

Diego has been featured in major media outlets, including CNN, NPR, Reuters, and The New York Times. His humanitarian work has earned him numerous awards, including:

  • Community Partner Award – NASW Arizona Chapter
  • Community Service Award – Southern Arizona Legal Aid, Inc.
  • Emerging Leader of the Year – NASW Arizona Chapter
  • Hispanic Heritage Month White House Invitee

A Commitment to Justice & Human Dignity

With a lifelong dedication to serving vulnerable populations, Diego’s mission is to ensure that every person, regardless of status or background, has access to dignity, security, and opportunity.
